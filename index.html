CSE 142 Analysis

Midterm Vs. Finals

The CSE142 class have two exams that is the midterm worth 20% and a final worth 40% of the total grade. This contitutes a total of 60% of the final grade. Plotting a scatter plot the scores on midterm vs. final shows us if the students perfomance on the final relative to the midterm.

Looking at the scatter plot we can conclude that majority of the class did not perform well on final as compared to the midterm.
